# SHA And Insurance Workflow

SHA workflows should connect invoices, claims, claim documents, payment method tracking, and reconciliation.

## Required records

- patient SHA details
- facility FID code
- invoice-to-claim link
- generated claim number
- claim status
- claimed amount
- approved amount
- rejected amount
- rejection reason
- resubmission history

## Reporting

- pending claims
- approved claims
- rejected claims
- claim value by date
- claim aging
- facility claim performance

Rejected SHA amounts should be reportable as losses unless recovered through resubmission or patient payment.
