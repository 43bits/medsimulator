# Reports And Printouts

Official documents must be generated by the backend where possible. Browser printing may remain as a convenience, but it must not be the only official export path for critical hospital records.

## Current Server-Side PDF Paths

- invoices
- payment receipts
- SHA claim forms
- IPD medical summaries
- IPD discharge summaries
- IPD treatment charts
- consultation medical reports

## Consultation Medical Reports

Endpoint:

```text
GET /reports/medical/consultations/:id.pdf
```

The endpoint requires authentication and applies facility/branch scoping before generating the file.

The PDF includes:

- facility/branch letterhead
- QR verification payload
- patient details
- consultation and appointment details
- triage snapshot
- clinical notes
- prescriptions
- lab orders/results
- clinician sign-off

Downloads are audited as `REPORTS/MEDICAL_REPORT_PDF_DOWNLOADED`.

## Printout Standards

Every official document should use:

- facility or branch logo where configured
- hospital name and contact details
- document title and number
- patient details where relevant
- prepared/served by details where relevant
- clean A4-safe margins
- readable black-and-white friendly tables
- totals/payment status where relevant
- QR or verification reference where useful
- no secrets, tokens, passkeys, or internal security data
