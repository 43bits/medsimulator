# Code of Conduct

This project is built for healthcare environments. Communication and contributions should be professional, direct, respectful, and focused on patient safety and system reliability.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat security and privacy concerns seriously.
- Discuss technical risk with evidence.
- Avoid exposing patient data, staff data, secrets, or production identifiers in public discussions.
- Keep reviews focused on correctness, safety, maintainability, and operational impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Publishing secrets or patient data.
- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Intentionally bypassing access controls.
- Encouraging unsafe clinical or payment behavior.

Maintainers may remove content or access that violates these expectations.
